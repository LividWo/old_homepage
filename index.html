Bio

Hi! I am a Fourth year PhD student in The University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Ben Kao. Previously, I received my B.Eng. degree from Wuhan University in 2017.

Interests

I am fascinated by the interpretability research in neural network models, with a focus on natural language processing. Recently, I am also interested in knowledge-grounded natural language understanding (e.g. KBQA) and generation (e.g. multimodal translation, knowledge-grounded dialogue system).
